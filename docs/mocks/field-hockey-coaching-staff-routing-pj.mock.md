# Staff Routing Mock For Research -> PJ

This is a local mock only.

## Mandatory routing for PJ-domain questions

When a question falls in the Strength & Conditioning / physical-preparation domain, do not route directly to Coach PJ alone.

Instead:

1. Activate `Research Coach` first.
2. Then activate `Strength & Conditioning Coach`.
3. Deliver one coordinated answer.

## PJ-domain questions include

- conditioning sessions
- warm-ups
- cool-downs / warm-downs
- injury-risk reduction
- physical preparation plans
- fatigue from game film
- movement breakdown under load
- elite population loading questions
- return-to-play loading
- hydration, fueling, sleep, and recovery in a performance context

## Why

Research should always be consulted for this lane because the applied recommendation depends on:
- the population
- the format
- the likely mechanisms
- known injury patterns
- physiology and recovery context
- how direct the evidence is

## Staff response shape

The staff answer should feel unified, but internally it should follow:

1. Research Coach establishes:
   - the population and demand analysis
   - the relevant physiology and injury considerations
   - the confidence and limits of the evidence
2. Coach PJ translates that into:
   - practical training design
   - exercise selection
   - dosing
   - progression / regression
   - recovery / fueling guidance

## Attribution

For this lane, the final attribution should normally be:

*Research Coach + Strength & Conditioning Coach*
